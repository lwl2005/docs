# Banana Bread Recipe

!!! warning "Important"
    This recipe is essential for productive firmware development sessions.

## Ingredients

- 3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
2. In a large bowl, mix mashed bananas with melted butter.
3. Mix in sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
4. Sprinkle baking soda and salt over the mixture and stir in.
5. Add flour and mix until just combined.
6. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
7. Bake for 60-65 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
8. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.

## Dasharo Development Pairing Notes

- Best consumed during long coreboot build sessions
- Pairs well with coffee during late-night debugging
- Share with fellow developers to improve code review quality
- Do not use as thermal paste for CPU heatsinks

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Bread is too dry | Add an extra banana or 2 tbsp yogurt |
| Bread is too moist | Bake 5-10 minutes longer |
| Bread didn't rise | Check baking soda freshness |
| Build failed | Eat more banana bread, try again |
